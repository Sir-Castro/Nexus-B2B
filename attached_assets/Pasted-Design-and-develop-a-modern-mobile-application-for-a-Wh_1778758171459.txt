Design and develop a modern mobile application for a Wholesale B2B E-commerce Platform that connects suppliers, manufacturers, distributors, and retailers in a seamless digital marketplace. The app should enable businesses to manage procurement, bulk purchasing, supplier relationships, and logistics directly from mobile devices.

The platform should support scalable enterprise operations while delivering a clean, intuitive, and high-performance mobile experience for Android and iOS users.

Core objectives:

Simplify wholesale buying and selling
Enable efficient bulk order management
Improve communication between suppliers and retailers
Provide secure and transparent B2B transactions
Support enterprise-scale commerce workflows

Key features:

User authentication and business onboarding
Secure registration and login
Business profile creation and verification
Role-based access for suppliers, retailers, logistics partners, and admins
KYC and supplier verification system
Supplier marketplace
Browse and search suppliers by category, location, rating, and industry
Supplier storefronts with catalogs and company profiles
Product filtering and advanced search
Featured and verified suppliers section
Product catalog and inventory management
Bulk product listings with variants and specifications
Inventory tracking and stock availability
MOQ (Minimum Order Quantity) management
Tiered pricing and wholesale discounts
Product images, videos, and downloadable brochures
Bulk ordering system
Add products in large quantities
RFQ (Request for Quotation) functionality
Repeat orders and saved procurement lists
Smart cart optimized for wholesale transactions
Multi-supplier checkout support
Negotiated pricing and credit terms
Real-time price negotiation between buyers and suppliers
Quote approval workflows
Flexible credit terms (Net 7, Net 30, Net 60)
Invoice generation and payment scheduling
Buyer credit scoring and spending limits
Logistics and delivery tracking
Real-time shipment and delivery tracking
Integration with third-party logistics providers
Delivery status notifications
Warehouse and fulfillment monitoring
Shipping cost estimation and route updates
Payments and financial management
Secure online payments
Multiple payment methods and currencies
Escrow or milestone payment support
Transaction history and receipts
Tax and VAT calculations
Communication and collaboration
In-app messaging between retailers and suppliers
Quote negotiation chat system
Push notifications for orders, deliveries, and payments
Business announcements and alerts
Analytics and reporting
Sales and procurement dashboards
Revenue and order analytics
Supplier performance metrics
Downloadable invoices and reports
Demand forecasting insights
Security and scalability
Enterprise-grade authentication and encryption
Cloud-based scalable infrastructure
Data backup and recovery
Admin moderation and fraud detection

Technical requirements:

Mobile-first responsive design
Android and iOS support
Offline support for saved catalogs and order drafts
Fast and scalable API architecture
Optimized performance for high-volume transactions

Suggested technologies:

Frontend: Flutter or React Native
Backend: Node.js, Django, or Laravel
Database: PostgreSQL, MySQL, or MongoDB
Cloud services: AWS, Google Cloud, or Azure
Payments: Stripe, Flutterwave, PayPal, or M-Pesa
Maps and logistics APIs: Google Maps API and shipping provider APIs
